Software for co-creating, accessing, and managing emergent events. It could be a meeting for a project, practice session for a martial art, gaming session, hobby hangout, or a big celebration. how to install the prototype

Although gather is just in its infancy, there is already a prototype that you can install and use!

  1. Download and install the Holochain Launcher for Windows, macOS or Linux.
    If you get stuck, you can find installation instructions in the release notes of the Holochain Launcher on GitHub.
  2. Start the Holochain Launcher and go through the setup process.
  3. Once started, click on "Install App" in the bottom right corner of the Holochain Launcher window. You will enter the App Library. Since Holochain is Peer-to-Peer it will take a while until data is synchronized with other peers. It depends on the speed of your internet connection and the speed of the internet connection of other peers.
  4. After a while you should see apps appearing in the App Library, one of which is the app "gather". Click on "Install".
  5. The default "App Id" is "gather", which is already good, but you can change it if you want. Leave the "network seed" field empty.
  6. Click on "Install".
  7. Now gather should show up in the list of apps and you can click on the Icon that shows "Open App" when you hover over it. You should now be good to go!
